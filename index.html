The Misfit Music of My Chemical Romance

By: Rebecca Santana

Photo by Stephen Eckert on Flickr. Attribution-NonCommercial-NoDerivs 2.0 Generic (CC BY-NC-ND 2.0)

My Chemical Romance made their debut at the height of the early 2000s emo renaissance. Their music has always spoken to the misfits of the world, tackling serious issues in some of their songs. The MCR fanbase loves the morbid, unsettling, unique nature of their music. My Chemical Romance broke up in 2013, but recently announced a reunion show, reigniting the emo in all of us. Get to know this iconic band and their quintessential music.
